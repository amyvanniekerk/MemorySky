Galaxy View Guide
=================

How the Galaxy Works
--------------------
Each memory you create becomes a star in your galaxy. Stars are colored by
emotion, sized by importance, and positioned by time and category. Related
memories in the same category form constellations (lines connecting the stars).

Interactions
------------
- Quick tap a star: opens the memory detail popup
- Long press + drag a star: rotates the entire galaxy like a wheel
- Pinch: zoom in/out
- Double tap: toggle zoom

Hidden Memories
---------------
You can hide a memory so it stays in the galaxy but is faded out. When a
memory is hidden:

- The star still appears in the galaxy but dimmed and greyed out
- Tapping a hidden star will NOT open the memory detail
- Instead, a small popup will show that the memory is hidden

To hide or unhide a memory:
1. Go to the Home screen
2. Tap the memory card to open the edit modal
3. Scroll to the "Visibility" toggle at the bottom
4. Tap to switch between "Visible in galaxy" and "Hidden from galaxy"
5. Save

Sharing
-------
Tap the share button (top right) to capture a screenshot of your galaxy.
The back and share buttons are automatically hidden from the screenshot
so you get a clean image of just your galaxy.
